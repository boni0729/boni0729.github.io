Café Marly occupies an ideal location under the arcades of the Louvre, a stone’s throw from the Carrousel and the Jardin des Tuileries. The restaurant’s Napoleon III-style interiors by the designer Olivier Gagnère provide a stylish yet warm setting, with both modern and classical decor features. There is a wonderful terrace seating area giving on to the Louvre’s iconic glass pyramid. The inventive cooking at this very trendy restaurant blends French and international flavours
